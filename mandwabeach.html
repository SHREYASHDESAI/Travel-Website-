Mandwa Beach

  • Location: Mandwa Beach is located on the Konkan coast of Maharashtra, India, near Alibaug.
  • Accessibility: Easily accessible from Mumbai via a ferry ride from Gateway of India, which takes approximately 1-2 hours.
  • Attractions: Known for its clean sandy shoreline, gentle waves, and scenic views of the Arabian Sea, making it a popular weekend getaway from Mumbai.
  • Water Sports: Offers various water sports activities such as jet skiing, banana boat rides, parasailing, and kayaking, providing exciting experiences for adventure seekers.
  • Beach Shacks: Lined with beach shacks and eateries serving delicious seafood, snacks, and refreshments, allowing visitors to relax and enjoy the coastal ambiance.
  • Cycling: Cycling is a popular activity on Mandwa Beach, with rental services available for bicycles, allowing visitors to explore the scenic coastline and nearby villages.
  • Beach Resorts: Several beach resorts and cottages are available near Mandwa Beach, offering comfortable accommodations with modern amenities for overnight stays.
  • Kolaba Fort: Explore the historic Kolaba Fort, situated in the sea waters near Mandwa Beach, accessible during low tide, offering panoramic views and insights into the region's maritime history.
  • Film Shoots: Mandwa Beach has been a popular location for Bollywood film shoots, attracting filmmakers and celebrities due to its scenic beauty and accessibility from Mumbai.
  • Scenic Sunset: Witness stunning sunset views over the Arabian Sea from Mandwa Beach, offering a tranquil and romantic setting for evening strolls and photography.
  • Weekend Retreat: Ideal for a day trip or weekend retreat from Mumbai, Mandwa Beach offers a refreshing escape from the city's hustle and bustle, with its serene ambiance and natural beauty.

Remember to check the weather conditions and tide timings before planning your visit for a safe and enjoyable experience.

Major Attraction:

  1. Alibaug Beach: A scenic beach destination located near Mandwa, known for its golden sands, clear waters, and water sports activities, including jet skiing, banana boat rides, and parasailing.
  2. Kihim Beach: Situated close to Mandwa, Kihim Beach is famous for its dense coconut groves, serene ambiance, and eco-friendly resorts, offering a perfect retreat amidst nature.
  3. Kolaba Fort: Explore the historic Kolaba Fort, located in the sea waters near Alibaug, accessible during low tide from Mandwa Beach, offering panoramic views and insights into the region's maritime history.
  4. Varsoli Beach: Varsoli Beach is another beautiful beach near Mandwa, known for its tranquil atmosphere, clean shoreline, and breathtaking views of the sunset, perfect for relaxation and leisurely walks.
  5. Water Sports Complex, Alibaug: Enjoy a variety of water sports activities at the Water Sports Complex in Alibaug, including jet skiing, kayaking, speed boating, and bumper rides, providing thrilling experiences for adventure enthusiasts.
  6. Murud-Janjira Fort: Visit the majestic Murud-Janjira Fort, located near Alibaug, built by the Siddis of Murud during the 17th century, offering historical significance, architectural grandeur, and panoramic views of the Arabian Sea.
  7. Awas Beach: Awas Beach is a secluded beach located near Mandwa, offering a peaceful environment, scenic beauty, and opportunities for birdwatching, making it an ideal spot for nature lovers and photographers.
  8. Kashid Beach: Kashid Beach is renowned for its pristine white sands, clear turquoise waters, and lush greenery, situated a short distance from Mandwa, offering a perfect escape for beach enthusiasts and nature lovers.
  9. Shiva Temple, Alibaug: Visit the ancient Shiva Temple in Alibaug, dedicated to Lord Shiva, known for its architectural beauty, spiritual significance, and religious festivals celebrated with great fervor.
  10. Mandwa Jetty: The Mandwa Jetty serves as the main gateway to Alibaug and nearby coastal destinations, offering ferry services to and from Mumbai, providing convenient access for tourists and travelers.
